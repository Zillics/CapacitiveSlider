Release 2016-11-27 (version 1).

Designed to perform as the General MIDI instrument #81: Synth Lead 1 (square).
It has been created with ZynAddSubFX / Yoshimi software synthesizers.

Sound samples and SF2 sound bank made by roberto@zenvoid.org for the FreePats
project: http://freepats.zenvoid.org/
